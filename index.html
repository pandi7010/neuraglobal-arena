The Problem

What’s broken in today’s engineering education

🧠 Theory Overload

Students are flooded with textbook knowledge but lack real-world application skills.

🔌 Lack of Practical Exposure

Most colleges don't have the infrastructure or faculty to offer hands-on experience.

📉 Skill Confidence Crisis

Students hesitate to repair, build, or design independently due to fear of failure or lack of practice.

🏫 Tier-2/3 College Disadvantage

Institutions outside major cities are left behind without advanced labs or modern tools.

My Story

When sadness turned into purpose.

Neura Global | ARena was founded on 11 July 2025, on a day when my close friends and I were deeply saddened by the skill gap in engineering education. That emotional moment sparked a powerful decision—I initiated this startup to make a real change.

ARena is more than just a platform; it's a mission to empower students with real practical experience using immersive AR/VR technology. Today, our journey has begun to rewrite how the world sees engineering education.
